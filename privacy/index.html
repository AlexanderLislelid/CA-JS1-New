Privacy Policy

At Square Eyes, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details: name, email address, billing information, and payment details.
  • Account information: login credentials and purchase history.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your purchases.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience.
  • To send important updates about your account or purchases.
  • For legal, compliance, and security purposes.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers (e.g., payment processors, hosting and analytics services).

Cookies and Tracking

Square Eyes uses cookies to provide a smoother browsing experience, store preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of our site may not function properly.

Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

Retention of Data

We keep your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.
